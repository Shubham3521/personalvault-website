FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you order.

What happens to my photos if PersonalVault shuts down?
▾

Your photos live on YOUR device, not our servers. Even if we shut down tomorrow, your photos are safely on the hardware you own, and the device continues to work completely independently.

How is this different from Google Photos or OneDrive?
▾

Cloud services store your photos on their servers and charge monthly. PersonalVault stores everything locally on a device you own — one payment, no recurring fees, and your data stays private and never leaves your home.

Can multiple family members use it?
▾

Yes. Create separate accounts for up to 5 family members. Everyone gets their own private space, with shared albums when you want them. Everyone in the family can back up independently.

What is the storage capacity and how does RAID work?
▾

You get 1TB of protected usable storage. PersonalVault uses two drives in RAID 1 mirroring — they automatically copy every photo to both drives in real time. If one drive fails, your memories are completely safe on the second drive. We'll send you a replacement drive and guide you through the swap.

How does remote access work without port-forwarding?
▾

PersonalVault uses an encrypted tunnel — similar to what VPNs use — to create a secure connection between your phone and your device at home. Your data passes through our edge relay infrastructure for routing, but is never stored or readable there. Think of it like a sealed tube — we move it, we never see it.

When will PersonalVault ship?
▾

We're shipping from June 2026. Early bird reservations will receive their units first, in order of reservation. You'll receive tracking updates via email as your order progresses.

Why don't you ask for my delivery address during checkout?
▾

We've kept checkout intentionally simple — just your name, email, and phone to secure your reservation. Once payment is confirmed, we'll email you a secure link to add your delivery address at your convenience. Your spot is locked in the moment you pay.

How do I contact support?
▾

Email us at support@personalvault.in — we respond within 24 hours on business days. We're a small, focused team and every support request goes directly to a real person.
