FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What happens to my photos if PersonalVault shuts down?▾

Your photos live on YOUR device, not our servers. Even if we shut down tomorrow, your photos are safely on the hardware you own, and the device continues to work.

How is this different from Google Photos or OneDrive?▾

Cloud services store your photos on their servers and charge monthly. PersonalVault stores everything locally on a device you own — one payment, no recurring fees, and your data stays private.

Can multiple family members use it?▾

Yes. Create separate accounts for up to 5 family members. Everyone gets their own private space, with shared albums when you want them.

What is the storage and redundancy?▾

1TB of protected storage — Two drives mirror your photos automatically. If one fails, your memories are safe.

How does remote access work?▾

Access from anywhere — View your entire library from any phone, anywhere in the world. No port-forwarding or router configuration required. Encrypted connections. Your data stays local.

How do I contact support?▾

Email support@personalvault.in — we respond within 24 hours on business days.

Why don't you ask for my delivery address during checkout?▾

We've kept checkout intentionally simple — just your name, email, and phone to reserve your vault. Once payment is confirmed, we'll email you a secure link to add your delivery address at your convenience. Your spot is locked in the moment you pay.
