Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PersonalVault

📦 Product

What is PersonalVault? expand_more
PersonalVault is a personal storage device that backs up your phone's photos, videos, and files automatically. It's like having your own private cloud — but you own the hardware. No monthly fees, no subscriptions.
What's included in the box? expand_more
  • Raspberry Pi 4 (4GB RAM)
  • 1TB hard drive
  • Power adapter
  • Ethernet cable
  • Quick start guide
How much storage do I get? expand_more
The standard model comes with 1TB of storage. That's enough for approximately 300,000+ photos or 500+ hours of HD video. You can also expand storage later by connecting external drives.

⚡ Setup & Usage

Is setup really that easy? expand_more
Yes! Just 4 steps:
  1. Plug the device into power and your router
  2. Download the PersonalVault app
  3. Scan the QR code on the device
  4. That's it! Backup starts automatically
Total time: About 5 minutes.
Do I need technical knowledge? expand_more
Not at all! If you can set up a WiFi router, you can set up PersonalVault. The QR code does all the hard work. We also include setup support if you need help.
Do I need constant internet? expand_more
The device needs to be connected to the internet to:
  • Access your files remotely (outside home)
  • Sync new backups
But your photos are stored locally on the device. Even without internet at home, you can connect and view them when you're away.
