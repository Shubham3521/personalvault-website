Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PersonalVault.

📦 Product
What is PersonalVault?
▾

PersonalVault is a plug-and-play personal storage device that backs up your family's photos and videos automatically. It's like having your own private Google Photos — but you own the hardware, pay once, and your data never leaves your home.

What's in the box?
▾

The box includes the PersonalVault device (Raspberry Pi + dual 1TB drives in RAID 1), power adapter, ethernet cable, quick start guide, and 1-year warranty card.

How much storage do I get?
▾

1TB of protected usable storage — enough for approximately 300,000+ photos or 500+ hours of HD video. Your storage is also mirrored across two drives automatically for safety.

Can my whole family use it?
▾

Yes! Up to 5 family members, each with their own private account. Everyone's backups stay separate, but you can create shared albums when you want.

⚡ Setup & Usage
Is setup really that easy?
▾

Yes — genuinely. Plug PersonalVault into power and your router, download the app, scan the QR code. Total time: 5 minutes. No IT knowledge required.

Do I need to configure my router?
▾

Nope. PersonalVault uses an encrypted tunnel for remote access — no port-forwarding, no router configuration, no dynamic DNS. Everything works automatically.

Can I access photos when away from home?
▾

Yes. View your entire library from any phone, anywhere in the world. Our secure encrypted connection keeps your data private while making it accessible wherever you are.

Which phones are supported?
▾

Android (8.0+) and iPhone (iOS 14+). Download the PersonalVault app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Automatic backup works for both platforms.

🔒 Privacy & Security
What happens to my photos if PersonalVault shuts down?
▾

Your photos live on YOUR device, not our servers. Even if we ceased to exist tomorrow, your photos remain safely on the hardware you physically own, and the device continues to work completely independently.

Does PersonalVault have access to my photos?
▾

No. Your photos are stored on your hardware at your home. They never touch our servers. Our encrypted relay tunnel connects your phone to your device — we route the connection, we never see or store the data.

How is RAID protection different from regular backup?
▾

PersonalVault uses two drives in RAID 1 — they mirror each other automatically, in real time. If one drive fails (which even good drives do eventually), your photos are 100% safe on the second drive. This is hardware-level redundancy, not a software backup.
