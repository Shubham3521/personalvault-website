Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Overview

PersonalVault is a self-hosted photo and video backup application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties. All data is stored exclusively on your own device and your own self-hosted server.

What the App Accesses

PermissionPurpose
Photo LibraryTo back up your photos and videos to your server
CameraFor QR code scanning during login
NetworkTo connect to your self-hosted PersonalVault server
NotificationsTo notify you of backup status
Background RefreshTo run automatic backups in the background

Data Collection

PersonalVault does not collect any personal data. Your photos, videos, login credentials, and server information are stored only on your device and your own server. We have no access to your data.

Analytics & Tracking

PersonalVault contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage in any way.

Your Server

PersonalVault connects only to the server URL you configure. We do not operate or control any servers. Your data goes directly from your device to your server.
