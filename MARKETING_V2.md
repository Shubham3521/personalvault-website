## 1. Brand Positioning (CRITICAL CHANGES)

### 1.1 Stop Calling It "Google Photos Alternative"

**Why:**
- Gives away your positioning to a competitor
- Makes you seem like a clone, not an innovator
- Google has massive marketing budget to own that narrative
- You're better than a "me-too" product

### 1.2 Your Actual Positioning

**Primary Messaging:**
> "PersonalVault - Your Private Family Photo Vault"
> "Backup photos to YOUR home, not the cloud"
> "The last photo backup solution you'll ever need"

**Taglines to Test:**
- "Your photos. Your hardware. Your control."
- "Private photo backup, made simple."
- "Family photos deserve family-level security."

**Differentiators to Emphasize:**
1. 🇮🇳 **Made in India** - Support local, serve local
2. 🔌 **Plug & Play** - No technical skills needed
3. 📱 **iOS + Android** - Both apps ready at launch
4. 🔒 **100% Private** - Your photos never leave your home
5. 💰 **One-time payment** - No monthly subscriptions ever

### 1.3 Competitor Positioning (Instead of Direct Comparison)

Don't say: "Better than Google Photos"
Do say: "For families who want privacy without the monthly fees"

Don't say: "Alternative to Immich/PhotoPrism"  
Do say: "For families who want self-hosted but can't set up a server"

## 4. "Notify Me" Strategy

### 4.1 Why You Need This

**Capture interested but not ready buyers:**
- Not everyone will buy immediately
- Some want to wait for reviews
- Others need to save money
- Some want to see it in person

**Benefits:**
- Build email list for launch
- Nurture leads over time
- Learn who interested buyers are
- Send targeted follow-ups

### 4.2 Implementation

**On Website:**
```
Form fields: Email (required), Name (optional)
CTA: "Notify Me"
Success message: "You'll be the first to know!"
```

**Follow-up Sequence (If They Don't Buy):**

**Email 1: Welcome (Day 0)**
> "Thanks for your interest in PersonalVault!"
> - What makes us different
> - Early bird offer details

**Email 2: Social Proof (Day 7)**
> "Here's what early adopters are saying"
> - Testimonials (if available)
> - Community growth

**Email 3: Launch Alert (Day 14-21)**
> "We're live! Last chance for early bird pricing"

**Email 4: Urgency (Day 27)**
> "Early bird offer ends in 3 days"

**Email 5: Post-Launch (Day 30+)**
> "We launched! Here's the full announcement"
> - Reviews/press coverage
> - Money-back guarantee reminder

### 4.3 Tools for Email Capture

**Free Options:**
- Google Forms → Google Sheets → Zapier → Email tool
- Mailchimp (free up to 500 contacts)
- ConvertKit (free up to 10 subscribers)

**Simple Implementation:**
```
Use Google Forms for now:
1. Create Google Form with: Name, Email
2. Link to email notifications
3. Export list periodically
4. Send manual follow-ups
```
